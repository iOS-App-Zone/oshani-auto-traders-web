About Oshani Auto Traders

Oshani Auto Traders (PVT) LTD is a trusted name in the automotive industry, specializing in direct imports of quality motor vehicles from Japan, Thailand, and India. With over 20 years of experience, we have built a reputation for excellence, reliability, and customer satisfaction. Our commitment to quality and transparency has made us one of the most trusted vehicle importers in the region.

  • Direct importers from Japan, Thailand, and India
  • Over 20 years of trusted service since 2003
  • Quality vehicles across all categories
  • Comprehensive vehicle information and pricing
  • Professional service with customer satisfaction commitment
